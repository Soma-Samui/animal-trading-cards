Monkey

Monkeys have their own unique set of fingerprints just like humans do.
They have a brain that is large for their size and it is part of the reason why they are so intelligent.

  • Scientific name: Macaca Fascicularis
  • Life span: 15-30 years
  • Avarage length: 1 metere
  • Habitat: tropical rainforests

Some organizations train capuchin monkeys as service animals to assist quadriplegics and other people with severe spinal cord injuries or mobility impairments.
After being socialized in a human home as infants, the monkeys undergo extensive training before being placed with disabled people. Around the house, the monkeys assist with daily tasks such as feeding, fetching, manipulating objects, and personal care.
